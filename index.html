Botcord

Botcord is a visual representation of OAuth applications in Discord, used to listen to a channel, chat as a bot, using a Node webserver.

Our goal

We strive to reach full Discord experience for OAuth applications, with the resources of Discord API, rebuild visual style, easy-to-manage authentication.
